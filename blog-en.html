Learn how to calculate pregnancy weeks and their number in months

30 January 2022 115 2068 Share Article

New mothers are often confused in the correct method of calculating the pregnancy according to the weeks and months, and most doctors usually resort to the method of calculating the weeks, and rely on it, whether in the mother’s file or the ultrasound and the follow-up of the pregnancy, and to get rid of this confusion, here is all the information that will help you in knowing the method of calculating the pregnancy Correct, and easily calculate pregnancy weeks by months

Correct way to calculate pregnancy

When thinking about the method of calculating the pregnancy, you should know some information and answers to some questions at the beginning, including:

  • - How many weeks of pregnancy?
  • - When was your last menstrual period?
  • - How are pregnancy weeks converted into months?
  • - Is pregnancy calculator available in months?

How many weeks of pregnancy?

The normal period of pregnancy is 40 weeks, and it may increase or decrease by two weeks, that is, it is between 38 and 42 weeks, and the period is measured in weeks, because the months are different, including more than four weeks. Pregnancy is calculated starting from the date of the first day of a woman's last menstrual period, as fertilization usually occurs between the tenth and fourteenth day of the menstrual cycle.

How are pregnancy weeks converted into months?

We also mentioned that the normal period of pregnancy weeks is 40 weeks, and it may increase or decrease by two weeks, that is, it is between 38 and 42 weeks, and the period is measured in weeks because the months are different, including more than four weeks.

  • The first stage of your pregnancy - the first three months: It starts from the first week until the end of the thirteenth week of pregnancy.
  • - The second stage of your pregnancy - the second three months: It starts from the fourteenth week until the end of the twenty-sixth week of pregnancy.
  • The third trimester of your pregnancy - the last three months: Starting from the twenty-seventh week until birth.
